    BURGER 🍔 INGREDIENTS

  • 🍞 Burger buns
  • 🍖 Ground beef patty
  • 🧀 Cheese slices
  • 🥬 Lettuce
  • 🍅 Tomato slices
  • 🧅 Onion rings
  • 🥒 Pickles
  • 🍅 Ketchup
  • 🌭 Mustard
  • 🥫 Mayonnaise

    🍔 Steps to Make a Burger

  1. 🍖 Prepare the ground beef and form it into patties.
  2. 🔥 Grill or fry the beef patty until cooked to your liking.
  3. 🍞 Toast the burger buns on a pan or grill.
  4. 🧀 Place a slice of cheese on the patty to melt.
  5. 🥬 Add lettuce on the bottom bun.
  6. 🍅 Add tomato slices on top of the lettuce.
  7. 🧅 Place some onion rings and pickles on the patty.
  8. 🍅 Add ketchup, mustard, or any other sauces you like.
  9. 🍞 Close the burger with the top bun.
  10. 🍽 Serve and enjoy your delicious burger!
