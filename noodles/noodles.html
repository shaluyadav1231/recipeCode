let's create NOODLES

INGREDIENTS

  • 🍝 Noodles
  • 🥕 Vegetables (carrot, cabbage, bell pepper)
  • 🥢 Soy sauce
  • 🧄 Garlic
  • 🫚 Ginger
  • 🌶️ Chili sauce
  • 🛢️ Oil
  • 🧂 Salt
  • ⚫ Pepper

steps to make NOODLES

  • 💧 Boil water in a pot.
  • 🍝 Add noodles to the boiling water and cook until soft.
  • 🧄🥕 Chop garlic, ginger, and vegetables (carrot, cabbage, bell pepper).
  • 🛢️ Heat oil in a pan.
  • 🧄 Sauté garlic and ginger in the hot oil until fragrant.
  • 🥕 Add the vegetables and stir-fry until they are slightly cooked.
  • 🥢 Add soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper to the vegetables.
  • 🍝 Drain the boiled noodles and add them to the pan.
  • 🍳 Toss everything together and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  • 🍽️ Serve hot and enjoy your delicious noodles!
