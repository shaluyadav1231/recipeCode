    Steps to Make Momo 🥟


  1. 🧄 Prepare the filling: Mix ground meat or veggies with garlic, onion, carrots, and spices.

  2. 🥟 Make the dough: Combine flour, water, and salt to form a smooth dough.

  3. 🍲 Roll the dough: Cut small pieces of dough and roll them into thin circles.

  4. 🥟 Fill the momo: Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each dough circle.

  5. 👩‍🍳 Fold and shape: Pinch the edges of the dough to seal the momo in a round or crescent shape.

  6. 🥘 Steam the momo: Place the filled momo in a steamer and cook for 15-20 minutes until done.

  7. 🍴 Serve: Enjoy your momo with spicy dipping sauce!

    Ingredients for Momo 🥟


  • 🥩 Ground Meat (Chicken, Pork, or Veggies)

  • 🧄 Garlic (finely chopped)

  • 🧅 Onion (finely chopped)

  • 🥕 Carrot (grated)

  • 🌿 Cilantro (chopped)

  • 🌶️ Chili (optional, for some spice)

  • 🥟 Dough (for wrapping)

  • 🧂 Salt and Pepper (to taste)
